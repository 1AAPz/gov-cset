The RRA model encourages ransomware readiness in three stages – Basic, Intermediate, and Advanced.

Intermediate
Basic

The RRA levels and associated practices across goals are intended to build from one level to another. It is recommended for an organization to review the associated practice at higher levels before beginning implementation. For some practices it may be worth while to implement the advanced practice while implementing the basic practice for others. As an example: most organizations will want to implement basic practices, but for the remote access they may wish to implement the more advanced two-factor authentication.
